About me

Hi, I’m Will

I design, research, write, and prototype for digitial products. I understand people and relationships to make things that solve problems.

Currently I design prototypes for large-scale websites at SapientNitro for clients like Panera Bread, AlzForum.org, and others. I am also a student of mindfulness meditation.

Recently I taught Interaction Design at Massachusetts College of Art and Design GDC Program and presented “UX Tools of the Trade” at the UXPA 2013 Boston Conference.

Previously I graduated from Massachusetts College of Art and Design’s Graphic Design BFA, during which I had three design internships, including at Mullen.
