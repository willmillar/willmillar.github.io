I research, sketch, style, write, and code for digitial product design. I have my feet on the ground, head in the sky*, and I want to make awesome things with a positive impact.

Currently I am an Experience Designer at SapientNitro working on websites, apps, and in-store experiences for clients like Panera Bread, Analog Devices, and AlzForum.org. I am also an active student of mindfulness meditation.

Recently I taught Interaction Design at Massachusetts College of Art and Design, presented “UX Tools of the Trade” at the UXPA 2013 Boston Conference, and guest lectured on information design for undergrad marketing students.

Previously I graduated with honors from Massachusetts College of Art and Design’s Graphic Design BFA. In that time I had three internships, including at Mullen. I was also part of the winning team at the 2013 Tufts/Evernote Hackathon.
